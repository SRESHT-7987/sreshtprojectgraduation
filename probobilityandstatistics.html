Probability and Statistics

Data can be represented in the form of graphs and charts.
We have learnt Pie Chart, Bar Graph, Pictograph and Probability in this grade.

Data representation relates to analyzing data, grouping or ogranizing data to present it better.
Pie Chart : In this type of representation, a Pie is divided into different parts where each part of the pie relates to unique data.


Bar Graph : This graphichal data representation is in the form of dual axis : x-axis and y-axis. The scale on x axis and y axis is divided into equal intervals and labelled accordingly. The graph can be given a title & each axis is labelled.

Pictograph : This graphical data representation, has each picture/symbol which follows a similar legend and key is provided to decode it.The graph is titled and labelled to analyze the data.

Probability : This is data analysis. Interpreting data and finding the most likely/less likely/Impossible/unlikely and equal chance cases after data is analyzed.
