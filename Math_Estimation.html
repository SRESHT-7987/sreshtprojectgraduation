What is estimation?

Estimation is the art of guessing and finding a value near to the actual. For ex: we estimate the time looking at the position of the sun in the sky
We regularly estimate various aspects in our day to day life. This topic deals with the art of estimation

Types Of Estimation

  • Our day to day dealings of life such as weight, height, distance etc which are estimated based on experience
  • Estimating a number by rounding it up to the nearest place value
  • Estimatimating the result of mathematical operations such as adition, multiplication etc

    Accuracy Of Estimation

  • The accuracy of estimation depends on the place value at which we perform the estimation such as tens, hundreds, thousands,etc.

    Few Examples

  • The distence from my home to school is approximatly 10km (Estimated value)
  • The number 54689124 when rounded of to nearest thousands is 54689100
  • 503x202 is estimatedly around 100000 (503 and 202 rounded to 500 and 200 respectively )
