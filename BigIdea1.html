Big Idea 1

All the matter in the universe is made up of small particles
Key Points:
Atom : The smallest particle of chemical that exists and the basic unit of life.
Molecule : A group of atoms that are bonded together.
Matter : Any substance living/non-living thing.
Material : Any substance that is made up of matter and anything that we can touch.
Particle : A very small unit of matter
Element : Anything which has same number of protons in the atomic nuclei
Compound : Any two or more elements make a compund
Mixture : A substance that is made by mixing atoms/elements

Most scientists have spent their effort in discovering more on Atomic structure and have come up with various atomic theories.
Some of the important contributions are :
Dalton's atomic theory :
1. All matter is composed of extremely small particles called atoms.
2. Atoms cannot be subdivided further. They can neither be created nor destroyed.
3. Atoms of same element are identical in size, mass and other properties. While, atoms of different elements differ in size, shape, mass and other properties.
4. Atoms of different elements can comine together to form compounds.
5. During chemical reactions, atoms are combined, separated or re-charged.

Modern atomic theory
1. Elements are composed of atoms, which are extremenly small.
2. All atoms of a given element have same chemical properties and contain same number of protons
3. Compounds are formed by the chemical combination of two or more different kinds of atoms.
4. Atoms are the units of chemical change.
