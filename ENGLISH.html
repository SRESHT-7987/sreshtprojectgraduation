What does inference means ? Inference is a guess that you make an opinion that you form based on the information that you have.
An inference is an idea or conclusion that's drawn from evidence and reasoning. An inference is an educated guess..
When you make an inference, you're reading between the lines or just looking carefully at the facts and coming to conclusions.
Extrapolate means ..
To project, extend or expand (known data or experience) into an area not known or experienced so as to arrive at a usually conjectural knowledge of the unknown area.
To make connections from known to the unknown.
